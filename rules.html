RULES OF THE MAZE

How to Navigate

Each level has 4 coloured doors — Red, Blue, Green, Yellow.
Four guides (A, B, C, D) will give you clues. Each may tell partial truth or lie — analyse wisely.
Only one door per level progresses the game. Others loop or send you backward.

Important Warnings

No retries. One wrong move can trap you forever.
No visual clues on doors. Only guide statements and level scenes help you decide.
Trust no one blindly. Even the most honest guide may mislead once.

Solution Path

Level 5 → Level 8 → Level 13 → Level 17 → Level 26 → Level 29 → Level 30
Remember it, this will help you to come out of the maze.
